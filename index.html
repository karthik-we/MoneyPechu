Who are we?

Mr. Anand Srinivasan started the "Money Pechu" YouTube channel as a platform to eradicate the economic myths and pessimistic thoughts on investment from the typical working-class people.

With all the efforts of Mr. Anand Srinivasan, this YouTube channel has been started on September 8th, 2018. His efforts are uplifted with technology assistance by his brother Mr. Vinoth. Today, Money Pechu extended its presence in all the other social media platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Telegram, and with more than 4,00,000 YouTube subscribers.

Today with the support of a technician team, Money Pechu turned into a people's media of financial advice. It delivers multi-dimensional information on the Market Economy, Tips on daily expenses, Financial advice to the common people, History of the Indian Economy and World Economy, and more economic contents in Tamil.
